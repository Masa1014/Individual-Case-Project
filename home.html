About JAPAN

This website contains information about Japanese culture, food and attractions !!

Japan has a population of about 127 milion people.


Japan consists of 5 main groups of islands;
Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu and Okinawa,
and Tokyo is the capital city of Japan.


Japan has four seasons and each season different
climate.




More details

  • Currency is Japanese Yen.

  • The Tokyo-Yokohama Metropolitan Greater Tokyo Area, home to 37.84 million people, is the most populous city in the world.

  • There are about 3000 McDonald's stores in Japan. This number is second only to the United States.

  • Japan has the second lowest "murder rate" in the world. However it is also a country with many suicides.

  • In Japan, eat Kentucky Fried Chicken for Christmas
