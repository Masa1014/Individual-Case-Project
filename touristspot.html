How many sightseeing spot in Japan do you know?

There are many attractive sightseeing spots in Japan.
On this page, I would like to introduce you to some of the attractive tourist spots
in Japan that I have carefully selected.

1.Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and the political, economic and cultural
center of Japan. Tokyo Sky Tree and Tokyo Tower are some of the tallest towers
in the world, and there are many shopping spots such as Shibuya, Harajuku,
and Omotesando.There is also Tokyo Disneyland, so I am sure you will have fun !!


2. Osaka

Osaka is the largest city in western Japan and is a great place to enjoy a unique urban atmosphere. There are many attractive spots such as Dotonbori, Osaka Castle, Kaiyukan (the world's largest aquarium), Universal Studios Japan, and Umeda Sky Building Observatory. In addition, Osaka has a well-developed food culture and It is especially famous for takoyaki and okonomiyaki.


3. Kyoto

Kyoto has been the official capital of Japan for over 1,000 years and is one of the most important destinations to experience traditional Japanese culture. There are many historic buildings such as Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kiyomizudera Temple, Kinkakuji (Golden Pavilion), Ryoan-ji Temple, Gion (geisha district).
