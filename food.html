What is your favorite Japanese food ?

Speaking of Japanese food that foreign tourists visit and praise in Japan, there are various dishes from popular and famous Japanese food such as Sushi and Teriyaki to unique food that you do not know. On this page, I will introduce seven Japanese foods that you should definitely try.


1.Ramen

Ramen is highly regarded by foreign tourists. The reasons are "delicious" and "fresh ingredients", "dishes that cannot be tasted in your own country" and "traditional and unique to Japan". At ramen shops all over Japan, you can enjoy unique and unique tastes, and the good cost performance is another reason why it is popular with foreigners.


2. Tempura

Like sushi, tempura is a popular food among Japanese people. empura is made by covering fish and vegetables with a special tempura dough and fried until it becomes crispy and pale golden. Also, vegetarians can eat it, so it is a highly recommended food.


3. Udon

The Japanese food I would like to recommend to you most is udon, because I think udon is one of the most healthy and reasonably priced Japanese foods. Udon is thick and chewy, traditionally made from flour and salt water, and the soup uses seafood and chicken. Also there are many types of udon, but the one I recommend most is beef udon.





Sushi Sushi is one of the first foods that comes to mind when thinking about Japanese food. I think maybe everyone has eaten it once. We promise that you will definitely be able to eat fresher, cheaper and higher quality sushi in Japan than in your country.
WAGYU Currently, the popularity of "WAGYU" is increasing especially in foreign countries. WAGYU is Japanese beef, and its representative brand is Kobe beef. Typical ways to eat Wagyu are "Shabu-shabu," "Yakiniku," "Steak," and "Teppanyaki." WAGYU is not cheap, but it is definitely a high quality food, so please give it a try !!
Okonomiyaki Okonomiyaki is made by mixing together batter, sliced cabbage, and other savory ingredients; spooning the mixture onto a hot plate; and then pan-frying as you would a pancake. Some restaurants allow you to cook your own okonomiyaki, so you can have a fun and enjoyable cooking experience.
Wagashi Japanese Traditional Sweets (Wagashi) are traditional Japanese sweets that are typically enjoyed in combination with a cup of green tea. The taste and shape of wagashi is very unique and you can enjoy it just by looking at it without eating it. It is also a popular souvenir.
